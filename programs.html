Cardio Training

Boost your endurance with our advanced cardio equipment and group classes.

What's Included:

  • Treadmill and elliptical machines
  • Cycling and spinning classes
  • High-energy aerobics sessions
  • Heart rate monitoring

Benefits: Improved cardiovascular health, increased stamina, weight loss, and better mood.

Strength Training

Build muscles and improve power with weightlifting and resistance programs designed for all fitness levels.

What's Included:

  • Free weights and resistance machines
  • Personalized strength programs
  • Group strength classes
  • Professional trainer supervision

Benefits: Increased muscle mass, improved endurance, enhanced metabolism, and bone health.

Duration: 45-75 minutes per session

Yoga & Flexibility

Relax your mind and body with yoga and stretching classes suitable for all levels, from beginner to advanced.

What's Included:

  • Hatha and Vinyasa yoga sessions
  • Guided stretching routines
  • Breathing and meditation practice
  • Flexible class schedules

Benefits: Better flexibility, reduced stress, improved posture, and enhanced mental focus.

Duration: 60 minutes per session

Swimming

Improve stamina and overall health with our swimming sessions and aqua aerobics, available for all skill levels.

What's Included:

  • Lap swimming and freestyle training
  • Aqua aerobics classes
  • Professional coaching sessions
  • Access to sauna and showers
